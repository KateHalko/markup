Aesthetic gymnastics



Aesthetic gymnastics is a sport based on stylized, natural movements of the whole body.
This sport includes harmonic, rhythmic and dynamic elements performed with natural grace and strength.
There is a continuous flow from one movement to another, as if every new movement came from the previous one.
All movements should be carried out smoothly, significantly changing both in dynamics and in speed.
The composition should contain universal and diverse body movements, such as body waves and swings, jumps and jumps, spiral turns and dance steps.
This requires flexibility, speed, strength, coordination and the ability to move without effort.
Various options for support and interaction of gymnasts are allowed and welcome.
